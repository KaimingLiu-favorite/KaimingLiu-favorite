Research Interests

Glycosylation in Tumor

Investigating the role of aberrant glycosylation(Especially O-GalNAc and O-GlcNAc) in tumor progression and metastasis, with a focus on developing glycobiology-based diagnostic and therapeutic strategies.

Gene Regulation

Exploring epigenetic modifications and transcriptional regulation mechanisms in cancer cells, aiming to identify novel targets for precision medicine.

Nucleic Acid Drug Delivery

Designing and optimizing nanocarriers for efficient delivery of siRNA, mRNA, and CRISPR-Cas9 systems to target cells for therapeutic applications.

Cancer Immunology

Studying the interaction between tumor cells and the immune system to develop innovative immunotherapies for hard-to-treat cancers.

Biomarker Discovery

Utilizing multi-omics approaches to identify novel biomarkers for early cancer detection and treatment response prediction.

Precision Medicine

Developing personalized therapeutic approaches based on individual genetic and molecular profiles for improved treatment outcomes.
